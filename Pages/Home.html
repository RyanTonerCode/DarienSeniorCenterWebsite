Welcome to Darien Mather Senior Center!

At the Darien Senior Programs , our mission is to nurture the physical, intellectual, and social well-being of the senior community aged 55 or better by providing a comprehensive and coordinated system of services that enhance individual lives, promotes well-being, dignity and independence throughout the aging process and keep members connected and integrated into the community by collaborating with other agencies and organizations who provide support programs and social services to senior, thereby enhancing the independence and wholesome lifestyle for seniors in Darien.

Our programs are evolving - please refer to our monthly newsletter for the most up to date program offerings.
