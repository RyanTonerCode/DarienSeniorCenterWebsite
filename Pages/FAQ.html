How old do you have to be to join?

Is there a membership fee?
Currently Mather Center does not charge a membership fee.
What activities do you offer?
Mather Center offers a myriad of programs that nurture the physical, intellectual and social well-being of the senior community.
A full schedule of our classes and programs can be found on line or in our newsletter.
Do you offer a lunch program?
Yes! Mather Center has a daily lunch offering for $5.00. Please reserve your lunch by 11:15am the day you plan on dining with us. Menus can be found on line or by calling the center.
