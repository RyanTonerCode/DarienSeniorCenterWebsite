Welcome to the Darien Mather Senior Center Nutrition Page!

Our Head Chef Judi Rotar

Judi is a classically trained Escoffier-Style French chef and holds certification as a ServSafe Food Protection Manager. Throughout her 30 plus years of being a chef Judi has practiced her passion in restaurants, as a caterer, and as well as a private chef. Judi enjoys the opportunity to prepare fresh healthy lunches at the Mather Center. Please come and dine with us!
