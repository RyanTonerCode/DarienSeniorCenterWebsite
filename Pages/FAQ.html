FAQ's

Where Are You Located?
2 Renshaw Road Darien, CT, 06820
Located Near the Darien Library
What Are Your Hours?
9:00am -3:00pm Monday through Friday, closed on holidays.
How Can I Contact the Center?
Phone: (203) 656-7490
How old do you have to be to join?
55 years old
Is there a membership fee?
Currently, the Mather Center does not charge a membership fee.
What activities do you offer?
The Mather Center offers a myriad of programs that nurture the physical, intellectual and social well-being of the senior community.
A full schedule of our classes and programs can be found online or in our newsletter.
Do you offer a lunch program?
Yes! Mather Center has a daily lunch offering for $5.00. Please reserve your lunch by 11:15am on the day you plan on dining with us. Menus can be found online, or by calling the center.
What are the center services?
  • Ask a CPA
  • Ask a Lawyer
  • Ask a Social Worker
  • Ask a Nurse (offered during blood pressure screening hours.) Also, blood-pressure readings are taken on the first Tuesday morning of each month 9:30-11:00 (except from June to September) and the third Tuesday of each month 12:30-2:00.
    This service is offered through the visiting Nurse and Hospice of Fairfield County.
  • Caregiver Educational Support Group the 4th Wednesday of each month 1:00- 2:30.
